How to give feedback

Tap the red ⏻ button in the top-right corner — it's always visible, no matter which screen you're on. Your feedback is used by AutoLoop to automatically improve the product.

AutoPMF is a structure that enables you to publish an AI service and control the AI service behaviour through a single Markdown file (mote.md).

After a test user tries the product, they send feedback — including an NPS score. The AutoLoop reads that feedback, generates an improved version of the control file, and publishes the update automatically.

The loop repeats every 10 minutes or every 10 reviews (customisable), continuously optimising the product until the best possible Product Market Fit is achieved for the prototype.
